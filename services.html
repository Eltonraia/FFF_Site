Faith Family Fellowship

At Faith Family Fellowship we want you to experience the Holy Spirit. Join us for coffee and fellowship at 9:30 am. Worship with us during a Holy Sprit led worship service followed by Pastor Bryan Cardin as he teaches from the Bible. Come as you are. Your life will never be the same in Jesus' name!

When do we gather ?

  • Sunday Morning at 10am
  • Wednesday evening at 7pm

Is there a place for my children ?


Sundays

  • Children's Church (ages 5-12)
  • Nursary (infants - age 5)

Wednesdays

  • Nursery (infants - age 5)
  • Girls Ministry (Girls age 5 - 12) Room 11
  • Royal Rangers (Boys age 5 - 12 years) Room 9
  • Chain Breakers Youth Group (Boys & Girls age 12 - 17) Youth Room

Our amazing greeters will help you check in, get your children settled in to their classes and show you where to go. Checkin opens at 9:30am on Sundays and 6:30pm on Wednesdays. We look forward to meeting you!
